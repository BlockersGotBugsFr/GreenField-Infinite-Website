About the Game & Devs

Green Field Infinite is a vast, open-world RPG where players can explore a dynamic and ever-changing landscape. Set in the mystical lands of Veridia, the game offers a rich narrative, stunning visuals, and deep character customization. Forge your own path and uncover the secrets of this beautiful yet treacherous world.

Meet the Developers

We are a small team of passionate developers working to bring our vision of a truly infinite world to life. Our team is dedicated to providing an immersive experience for all players.
